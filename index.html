The Artist

Unapologetically
Bold

Crystal Orlando's life and art intertwine through a deep connection to the natural world. Raised in rural Central Texas, her earliest companions were horses, rescued animals, and the wild creatures that shaped her sense of wonder.

She pioneered the technique of drawing large-scale charcoal and graphite works directly on canvas and cradleboard, a medium she chose not only for its accessibility, but for its unforgiving demand for precision. There is no painting over a mistake. Every mark is a commitment.

In 2020, Orlando faced a breast cancer diagnosis. Art became both sanctuary and strength. The same resilience that carried her through treatments now breathes through every stroke, subjects captured always moving forward, leaving the past behind.
